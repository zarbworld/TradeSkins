How to Buy and Sell Skins in Counter-Strike 2

This page lists a variety of CS2 Market sites, for buying CS2 skins and selling CS2 skins. There are a few reasons why it is better to use a 3rd party site for buying and selling CS2 skins, rather than using the steam community market.

The most obvious reason for using 3rd party CS2 market sites, is that you can trade your skins directly for real cash. When you sell skins on the steam community market, you only receive steam balance which can only be used to purchase games or other skins.

Another reason for using 3rd party CS2 market sites, is that skins come at a reduced cost. The price of skins can be upwards of 30% off compared to steam community market prices. This is because you can trade for real cash, and the fees are much lower compared to steam's 15% fee.

The best CS2 market site is CSFloat. It has the lowest fees, at only 2%, and has the biggest volume of items. It has become the best market for players outside of China, since BUFF163, which used to be the largest CS:GO marketplace, began requiring users to have a Chinese ID in May of 2024. CSFloat also has a robust database, allowing you to view unique crafts as well as a ranking system for floats.

It is important to use a variety of CS2 market sites when buying, as it increases the chances of finding the best deal.
